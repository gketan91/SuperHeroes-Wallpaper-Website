About us

Heros Wallpaper provides high quality and completely free stock photos licensed under the Heros Wallpaper license. All photos are nicely tagged, searchable and also easy to discover through our discover pages.

Photos

We have hundreds of thousands free stock photos and every day new high resolution photos will be added. All photos are hand-picked from photos uploaded by our users or sourced from free image websites. We make sure all published pictures are high-quality and licensed under the Heros Wallpaper license.

Only free images from our community of photographers and sources like Google and many more are added to our photo database. We constantly try to deliver as many high quality free stock photos as possible to the creatives who use our website.
